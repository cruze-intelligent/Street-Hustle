Street Hustle

Start with honest side jobs, build your name, unlock districts, and grow a resilient hustle empire one smart move at a time.

How To Play

  • Start with Wash Clothes and tap Hustle to build your first cash buffer
  • Upgrade and unlock new hustles until automation takes over routine income
  • Claim missions, achievements, and streak rewards to grow Street Cred
  • Use Street Cred and district goals to unlock bigger parts of Kampala
  • Choose specialization tracks and story-event decisions to shape how your empire scales
